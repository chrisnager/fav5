Fav 5 list

Pretend you had to move today and sell everything you owned except for the five most important items to you.* What would you hold on to?

My Fav 5 list would include:

  1. iPhone 5s
  2. Retina MacBook Pro
  3. Dyson vaccuum
  4. Acoustic guitar
  5. Snowboard

Share your Fav 5 list: Tweet

*The clothes and accessories you're wearing plus the chargers and accessories for your electronics are included.

Recently, I've been working on simplifying the way I live and came up with this Fav 5 exercise. Though, I don't have plans to live strictly with these five things, it has helped me put my stuff into perspective and purge some things I don't really need.
